Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What services do you offer?

I specialize in web development, including frontend development with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, as well as backend development with Python and PHP. I also create interactive games and data visualization dashboards.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity. A simple website might take 1-2 weeks, while a complex web application could take 1-3 months. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you work with students or small businesses?

Absolutely! As a student myself, I understand the needs of other students and small businesses. I offer competitive rates and flexible arrangements to fit different budgets and requirements.

What technologies do you work with?

I work with HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Python, PHP, and various frameworks and libraries. I'm also familiar with databases, version control (Git), and modern development tools.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes! I offer ongoing support and maintenance for all projects. This includes bug fixes, updates, and feature additions. We can discuss support packages that work best for your needs.

How do we get started?

Simply reach out through the contact form or email me directly. We'll schedule a consultation to discuss your project requirements, timeline, and budget. From there, I'll provide a detailed proposal.
